Welcome to the Theme Chooser.



The theme is applied to every page on your website in order to provide a consistent appearance to buyers.

Themes also decide which fonts, colors, and buttons appear on your site in addition to how your logo is displayed.

Zoovy includes over 450 themes that you may choose from. You may also purchase pre-built custom themes at http://proshop.zoovy.com, or commission Zoovy to build a custom theme for you.

If you have the "WEB" feature bundle on your account then you can create and upload your own custom themes.

If you are new to Zoovy we recommend you simply select any pre-built theme that appeals to you. You can always change it later. Zoovy will remember the themes you select and compile a list of them so it's easy to try different ones.

To get started, use the categories on the left. We recommend starting in the "User Favorites" or "Staff Favorites" lists since those are the most popular.
