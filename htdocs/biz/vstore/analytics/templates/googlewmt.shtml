Google SiteMap Configuration
Google SiteMaps URL: http://www.google.com/webmasters/sitemaps

This tool is used to verify your sites participation in Google SiteMap program, this will enable you to see additional reports and statistics to see which pages have been spidered for each of your domains.

Usage: Login to Google SiteMap, go to Verify, select "Add a Meta tag" for your Verification method. Cut and paste the full: 
<META name="google-site-verification" content="xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx=" />
You will need to provide the text field for each of your domains below, then press save. Then in the Google SiteMap administration check the box which says "I've added the META tag to the homepage" and press "Verify Now", this will cause Google's SiteMap to come to your store and verify your domain.
