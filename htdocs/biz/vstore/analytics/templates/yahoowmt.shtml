Use the area below to verify your sites participation in Yahoo Site Explorer program so you can see crawl errors, and other issues which may prevent your pages from being indexed. This will enable you to see additional reports and statistics to see which pages have been spidered for each of your domains.

Usage: Login to Yahoo Site Explorer (http://siteexplorer.search.yahoo.com), go to My Sites, Add a site, select "Meta TAG to my homepage" for your authentication method. Cut and paste the full: 
<meta name="y_key" content="...." />
Into the text field for each of your domains below, then press save. store and verify your domain.
