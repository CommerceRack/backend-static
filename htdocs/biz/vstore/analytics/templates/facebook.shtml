Facebook ()
Facebook is a popular method you can utilize to engage your customers. Any business can setup a "fan page" for free by going to: http://www.facebook.com/advertising/?pages

Facebook fan pages provide a highly reliable opt-in method to communicate to your "fans" (customers). In addition it gives your customers a myriad of tools to socially recommend your business & products to their friends. A Facebook fan page is a long term investment in your business.

Further - by leveraging Wishpot you can easily submit product feeds and updates to your clients automatically.

If you wish to enable Facebook Application (Login) support for this partition, you must do that in Setup / Plugins, and if you have multiple partitions and/or speciality sites we highly recommend reading webdoc to understand how these various components interact.

Facebook Fan Page URL:
Be sure to include the full URL to the facebook page ex: http://www.facebook.com/pages/Zoovy-e-Commerce/99488342473



Step 1: Add Facebook to your Sidebar
Once you have added Facebook URL be sure to add it to your sidebar in Setup > Website Builder > Sidebar.

Step 2: Add Facebook to your Checkout
name="facebook:chkout"> Include the Facebook graphic/link on the last page of Checkout.
